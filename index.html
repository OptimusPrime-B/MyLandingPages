Effective Date: 7/2/2026

CSNAWorks ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Money Exchange Locator mobile application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following specific types of data to provide core app functionality:

Email Address: Collected when you register or contact support. Used for account authentication, password recovery, and essential service notifications.

Phone Number: Collected optionally (or mandatorily, specify which) during profile setup. Used for account verification (2FA) and urgent service alerts.

Precise GPS Location: Collected automatically when the app is in use (foreground).Used to provide location-based services, such as displaying your location in map.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

App Functionality: To enable core features like user authentication and location tracking.

Account Management: To manage your profile and secure your account.

Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries via email or phone.

Safety & Fraud Prevention: To verify user identity and prevent abusive behavior.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party vendors who assist in app operations (e.g., cloud hosting providers like AWS/Google Cloud, or email delivery services). These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/SSL) and secure server storage, to protect your email, phone number, and location data from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

Correction: Update inaccurate information (e.g., changing your email or phone number in settings).

Deletion: Request the deletion of your account and all associated data. To do so, contact us at [Your Contact Email] or use the "Delete Account" feature within the app settings.

Opt-Out: You can disable GPS location collection at any time via your device's permission settings, though this may limit app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.
